pH Calculator (Strong & Weak Acids)

The pH calculator helps determine the acidity or basicity of a solution based on hydrogen ion concentration.

What is pH?

pH is defined as the negative logarithm of hydrogen ion concentration.

Formula:

pH = -log[H⁺]

If the hydrogen ion concentration is greater than 10⁻⁷ M, the solution is acidic. If it is less than 10⁻⁷ M, the solution is basic.

Example Calculation

If hydrogen ion concentration is 10⁻³ M:

pH = -log(10⁻³) = 3
